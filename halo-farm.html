What's Behind Our Low Prices?

Halo Farm was started by Jerry Reilly who had previously been president of a large food company. He saw first hand the exessive costs of trucking perishable foods for miles and miles. His idea was to build a dairy with a store attached - no trucks on the road. The concept was realized in 1975 - a modern dairy without trucks. The result LOW PRICES and higher QUALITY.


  • We improve freshness by several days.
  • Our milk is tastier because we buy from small family owned dairy farms.
  • We don't use undesireable additives. No stabilizers, no preservatives, no artificial colors, no artificial flavors.
  • We use very expensive ingredients and still keep our prices very, very low.
